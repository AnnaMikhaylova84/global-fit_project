180 Day Complete Makeover

This program is to implement health and fitness as an everyday lifestyle. We will help you in building a foundation of flexibility, core strength and cardiovascular health. We will also teach you healthy eating habits, what are the do’s and the don’ts, how certain foods will impact your health and progress. Included in the Program

1
Workout and Nutritional plans which change every 4 weeks
2
One 50 minute coach session per week
3
Daily text and email support
4
Detailed client tracker spreadsheets emailed to you monthly showing in detail your progress
5
A bioelectrical impedance analysis which is to help track your body composition. Your body composition is how much lean muscle mass you have vs your visceral fat (the fat around your organs) and your subcutaneous fat (the fat under your skin)
